Privacy Policy for Search Console Pro

Effective date: October 22, 2024

Thank you for choosing to use Search Console Pro. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data while using the Search Console Pro Chrome extension.

Data Collection

No data is collected. Search Console Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or search information. All interactions with the extension are processed locally within your browser, and no data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The permissions required by the extension are solely for enabling its functionality. Search Console Pro requires access to your browser's search capabilities to enhance the user experience, but this access is strictly local and does not involve any data collection or transmission outside your device.

Third-Party Services

Search Console Pro does not use or interact with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics tools that collect user data.

User Control

All settings and configurations within Search Console Pro are managed entirely by you. You can customize themes, keyboard shortcuts, and other features locally through the extension's settings page. These customizations are stored locally in your browser.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will be effective as of the date listed at the top of this document.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the use of your data, please feel free to contact us at privacy@dorpascal.com.
